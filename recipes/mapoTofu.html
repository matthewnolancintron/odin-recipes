Mapo Tofu


Archon6812, CC BY-SA 3.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Description

Tofu, minced meat and mushrooms in a spicy sichuan sauce topped with green onions.

Ingredients

  • Main ingredients
    • Tofu
    • ground meat of your choice
    • mushrooms
    • garlic
  • sauce
    • doubanjiang
    • chili oil
    • (optional) miso paste
  • toppings
    • chili flakes
    • sichuan peppercorns
    • green onions

Instructions

  • Prep
    • Main ingredients and toppings
      • Cut a block of tofu into cubes.
      • Mince 3 cloves of garlic.
      • Slice a few mushrooms
      • Chop up some green onions.
      • Mix the garlic into the meat.
    • The sauce
      • Put 1 or 2 tablespoons of doubanjiang into a bowl
      • Half as much miso paste as doubanjiang
      • teaspoon of chili oil
  • Cooking
    1. Pre heat the pan on mid for a minute
    2. Coat the pan with chili oil and let it heat up for a minute or two
    3. add white parts of the green onions to the pan and heat until golden
    4. add garlicy meat and cook until it's no longer pink-ish (should be a toasty brown)
    5. add the mushrooms and char them a tad
    6. drop in the tofu
    7. pour in the sauce
    8. sprinkle in some sichuan peppercorns
    9. let simmer on low for a bit
    10. top with the greens from the green onions
