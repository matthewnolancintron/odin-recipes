Zaru Soba

Description

refreshing soba noodles, with a mentsuyu dipping sauce and side of green onions.

Ingredients

  • Main ingredients
    • soba noodles
  • mentsuyu dipping sauce
    • sake
    • mirin
    • soy sauce
    • katsuobushi
  • toppings
    • green onions

Instructions

  • Make homemade mentsuyu sauce
    1. add a cup of sake and bring to a light boil
    2. add a cup mirin and continue to keep at a light boil for 2 mintues
    3. add a cup of soy sauce and katsuobushi flakes and simmer on low for 3 mintues
    4. take off heat and let cool for 9 mintues
    5. strain out katsuobushi flakes
    6. put sauce in fridge to cool while cooking the noodles
  • Cooking
    1. bring water to a boil
    2. add noodles and cook for 5 or 7 mintues
    3. strain noodles and rinse with cold water
    4. add noodles to ice water bath and let sit for 2 to 3 mintues
  • serving
    • top with the greens from the green onions or serve on the side on a small tray
    • pour sauce into a small dipping bowl to dip noodles into or pour sauce ontop of noodles
